Common Questions

Everything you need to know about joining TrueConnex.

Why is there a membership fee?
The fee serves two purposes: it covers the cost of our manual ID verification process (keeping you safe) and ensures that every member is financially invested in finding a serious connection, filtering out casual users and bots.
How does the verification process work?
After you register, our team manually reviews your details against government IDs and social profiles. This process typically takes 24-48 hours. Once verified, you'll receive your unique Member Code to unlock event details.
What happens if I don't meet anyone?
TrueConnex focuses on creating organic environments for connection. While we can't guarantee a match, we guarantee a room full of verified, eligible singles. Many members make great friends even if they don't find a partner immediately!
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Your personal contact details are encrypted and never shared with other members without your explicit consent. We use your ID solely for verification purposes and delete sensitive documents post-approval.
